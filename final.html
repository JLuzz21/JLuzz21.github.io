Welcome to my Final Project Page!

This is my very own final project website page. For my project I worked with my partner Matt Albanese. We decided to make a Asteroid Dodge game.
On the home page there is a nice display with both of our bitmoji's on the front. Also on the home page is an information button to learn more about the game.
In order to play the game you need to move the rocketship (the user) left and right to avoid the incoming asteroids. Upon impact from an asteroid you will lose 5 points.
But, every second that you stay avoiding asteroids you will gain a point. So stay away from the rockets, and watch your score increase!
